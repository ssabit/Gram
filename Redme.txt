-----------------------------------
			INSTRUCTION
-----------------------------------

To start game goto to,Gram/bin/Debug and double click the Gram.exe file.


-----------------------------------
			CONTROLS
-----------------------------------

KEYBOARD:

Left,Right,Up,Down	-Man/Alien Walk, navigate menus
C					-Cycle start
X					-Cycle stop
B					-Boat start
N					-Boat stop

MOUSE:

Left				-WindMill Spin left
Right				-WindMill Spin right
Wheel				-WindMill Spin stop
